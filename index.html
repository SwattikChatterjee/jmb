About Us

JM Biotech PVT LTD specializes in innovative solutions for sustainable construction and energy. The company harnesses the potential of microsilica and rice husk ash as sustainable & affordable additives in high-strength concrete, enhancing its durability and mechanical properties. Additionally, JM Biotech produces biomass pellets from rice husk, providing a renewable fuel alternative that supports cleaner energy initiatives. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, JM Biotech PVT LTD plays a vital role in the construction industry, contributing to the development of skyscrapers and bridges while promoting environmentally responsible practices.

Established in 1996, we have been committed to making a positive impact in our industry. Whether you are a small business or a large corporation, we have the expertise to help you succeed.



Biomass Pellets

Biomass pellets are small, cylindrical pieces of compressed organic material, primarily made from wood, agricultural residues, and other plant-based materials, offering a renewable energy source for heating and power generation.

MicroSilica

Microsilica, or silica fume, significantly enhances the strength of concrete by filling microvoids and improving the overall density of the mix. The incorporation of microsilica also increases resistance to shrinkage and cracking, making it ideal for high-performance concrete applications.

Rice Husk Ash

Rice husk ash is a byproduct of burning rice husks, which are the outer coverings of rice grains, and it is rich in silica, making it a valuable material in construction and agriculture. When used as a pozzolanic additive in concrete, rice husk ash improves strength, durability, and workability while reducing the environmental impact by recycling agricultural waste.
