HR Policy


Welcome to JM Biotech PVT LTD


At JM Biotech, we are committed to fostering a work environment that promotes fairness, respect, and equal opportunities for all employees. Our HR policies are designed to support our mission of manufacturing high-quality Microsilica for high-strength concrete, Biomass Pellets as a fuel alternative, and Rice Husk Ash for thermal insulation.


Equal Employment Opportunity

JM Biotech PVT LTD is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, or any other status protected by applicable law.


Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a professional manner at all times. This includes treating colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with respect and integrity.


Workplace Safety

We prioritize the safety and well-being of our employees. All employees must adhere to safety protocols and report any unsafe conditions to their supervisor immediately.


Employee Benefits

JM Biotech offers a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off. Details of these benefits will be provided during the onboarding process.


Grievance Procedure

If an employee has a complaint or concern, they should follow the grievance procedure outlined in the employee handbook. We encourage open communication and will address concerns promptly and fairly.


Conclusion

We believe that our employees are our greatest asset. By adhering to these policies, we can create a positive and productive work environment for everyone at JM Biotech PVT LTD.

Quality Policy


Commitment to Quality


Our technical know-how & stringent control over production chain documented as per ISO 9001:2015 have enabled us to establish an internationally accepted qualities as demanded by the steel Mills all over the Globe.At JM Biotech PVT LTD, we are dedicated to delivering the highest quality products in the industry. Our commitment to quality is reflected in every aspect of our operations, from the sourcing of raw materials to the manufacturing and distribution of our products.


Quality Objectives

  • To consistently meet or exceed customer expectations for product quality and performance.
  • To implement rigorous quality control measures throughout our manufacturing processes.
  • To continuously improve our quality management system through regular audits and reviews.

Private Testing Laboratory

We operate a private testing lab equipped with advanced technology to conduct thorough testing on our products. This lab allows us to:

  • Perform detailed analysis of Microsilica, Biomass Pellets, and Rice Husk Ash to ensure they meet our stringent quality standards.
  • Conduct regular quality checks throughout the production process.
  • Provide rapid feedback and adjustments to manufacturing processes based on testing results.

Quality Assurance Practices

We adhere to strict quality assurance practices to ensure that our products meet the highest standards. This includes:

  • Regular testing and analysis of raw materials and finished products.
  • Employee training programs focused on quality control and best practices.
  • Feedback mechanisms to capture customer insights and improve our offerings.

Compliance and Certification

JM Biotech PVT LTD complies with all relevant industry standards and regulations. We are committed to obtaining and maintaining certifications that reflect our dedication to quality, including ISO 9001.


Continuous Improvement

We believe in the principle of continuous improvement. Our team regularly reviews processes and seeks innovative solutions to enhance product quality and operational efficiency.


Conclusion

Quality is at the core of everything we do at JM Biotech PVT LTD. We strive to be a leader in our industry by delivering products that our customers can trust and rely on.
